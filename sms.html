The Inus grant operates through a inus grant system, which is the primary mechanism for providing financial assistance for African development. This grant is central to the Inus framework as it serves as the main avenue through which funds are channeled to support various initiatives and projects aimed at fostering development in Africa. The focus of the Inus grant is specifically on leveraging the resources and expertise of the African blockchain to contribute to the economic, social, and infrastructural advancement of the continent. By mobilizing financial resources from the Inus community, the inus grant plays a crucial role in addressing key development challenges and unlocking opportunities for sustainable growth across Africa..
